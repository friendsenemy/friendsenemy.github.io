Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any fees or commissions when I sell?+

Absolutely not. There are zero fees, zero commissions, and zero closing costs on your end when you sell to Pages of Purpose LLC. The offer amount we present is the amount you receive at closing. No surprises, no deductions, no hidden charges.

What is the difference between you and a wholesaler?+

A wholesaler puts your house under contract and then sells that contract to another buyer — often at a lower price and with added delays. Pages of Purpose LLC buys directly. We have the cash, we close the deal ourselves, and you deal with one company from start to finish.

Do you buy houses in other states?+

Currently, our primary market is Maryland, with a focus on Silver Spring and surrounding areas. If you have a property outside of Maryland, contact us — we may be able to help directly or connect you with a trusted buyer in your area.

What happens after I submit the form?+

After you submit the form, a member of our team will contact you — usually within a few hours during business hours. We will ask a few follow-up questions about your property, explain the process in detail, and begin preparing your offer. There is never any obligation.

Is this a legitimate company?+

Pages of Purpose LLC is a registered Maryland company that buys properties directly from homeowners. We are not wholesalers — we buy and hold or renovate properties ourselves. We are happy to provide references from past sellers and proof of funds upon request.

What paperwork do I need to sell my house?+

We handle the vast majority of the paperwork. Generally, you will need a government-issued ID, your mortgage payoff information (if applicable), and any relevant documents like probate letters or divorce decrees. Our team guides you through everything.

Will you try to lowball me?+

No. Our offers are based on real Silver Spring market data and are designed to be fair. We are not looking to take advantage of anyone. If our offer does not work for you, there is no obligation and no hard feelings. We encourage you to compare our offer with other options.
