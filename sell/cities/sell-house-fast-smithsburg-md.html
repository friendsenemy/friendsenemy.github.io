Frequently Asked Questions

Do you buy commercial properties?+

Our primary focus is residential properties in Smithsburg — single-family homes, townhouses, condos, and small multi-family buildings. For commercial properties, please contact us to discuss — we may be able to help or refer you to a trusted partner.

What if I owe more than my house is worth?+

If you are underwater on your mortgage, you may still have options. In some cases, we can negotiate a short sale with your lender where they accept less than the full balance. Contact us to discuss your specific situation — we have experience with these negotiations.

Do I need to be present for the closing?+

Not necessarily. While many sellers choose to attend the closing in person, we can also arrange remote closings for sellers who have already relocated or prefer not to attend. We work with title companies that offer mail-away and virtual closing options.

How quickly will I receive an offer?+

In most cases, you will receive a written cash offer within 24 hours of contacting us. For more complex situations — such as properties with significant title issues — it may take an extra day or two while we complete our research.

What types of properties do you buy in Smithsburg?+

We buy single-family homes, townhouses, condos, duplexes, multi-family properties, mobile homes, and vacant land throughout Smithsburg. Occupied or vacant, good condition or distressed — we buy it all.

Can I sell an inherited property?+

Yes. We buy inherited and probate properties throughout Smithsburg. Whether you are the sole heir or one of several, we work with you and your attorney to make the process smooth. In many cases, we can close while probate is still underway.

Do I need to clean out the house before selling?+

No. You can leave behind anything you do not want — furniture, appliances, personal items, junk, even vehicles. We handle all cleanout and disposal after closing at no cost to you.
