Frequently Asked Questions

How do you determine your offer price?+

We base our offers on real market data specific to New Market. We analyze recent comparable sales, current market conditions, the property's condition, and the cost of any needed repairs. Our goal is to make a fair offer that reflects the true value of your home.

How fast can I sell my house in New Market?+

With Pages of Purpose LLC, you can sell your New Market home in as little as 7 days. We pay cash, so there is no waiting for bank approvals. Most closings happen within 7 to 14 days, but we can accommodate any timeline that works for you.

What if my house is in a flood zone?+

We buy properties in flood zones throughout New Market. Flood zone designation can scare off traditional buyers due to mandatory flood insurance requirements, but it does not affect our willingness to purchase. We factor flood zone status into our offer and handle it accordingly.

Can I sell a house that is fire damaged?+

Yes. We buy fire-damaged properties in New Market. Whether the damage is cosmetic or structural, we assess the property and make a fair offer based on its current condition and the cost of restoration. You do not need to file insurance claims or make any repairs.

Can I change my mind after accepting an offer?+

We want you to feel completely confident in your decision. If you have second thoughts before closing, talk to us. We believe in doing right by our sellers, and we would rather lose a deal than have a seller feel pressured into something they do not want.

Can you buy my house if it has code violations?+

Yes. Code violations are common, especially on older or vacant properties in New Market. We buy properties with active code violations and handle the remediation after closing. You do not need to fix anything before selling.

Can I sell my house if it needs a new roof?+

Absolutely. A new roof is one of the most common — and most expensive — repairs homeowners face. We buy houses that need roofs, foundations, HVAC systems, plumbing, electrical, and everything else. You do not spend a dime on repairs.
