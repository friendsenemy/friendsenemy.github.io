A huge portion of the local housing market is tied to military families cycling through three-year tours, civilian defense contractors, and landlords who bought during past deployment cycles and are now ready to exit. Pages of Purpose LLC buys California MD homes directly for cash, whether you're facing PCS orders with 60 days to clear out, selling a tired rental, or settling an inherited property in St. Mary's County.

California is unincorporated St. Mary's County. Foreclosures go through the St. Mary's County Circuit Court in Leonardtown, and probate is handled by the St. Mary's County Register of Wills. The market is heavily renter-occupied, driven by Pax River personnel rotations, which means a large percentage of homeowners here are investors rather than owner-occupants.
