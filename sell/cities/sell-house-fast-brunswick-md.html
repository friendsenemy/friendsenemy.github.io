Yes. We buy fire-damaged properties in Brunswick. Whether the damage is cosmetic or structural, we assess the property and make a fair offer based on its current condition and the cost of restoration. You do not need to file insurance claims or make any repairs.

Can I change my mind after accepting an offer?+

We want you to feel completely confident in your decision. If you have second thoughts before closing, talk to us. We believe in doing right by our sellers, and we would rather lose a deal than have a seller feel pressured into something they do not want.

Can you buy my house if it has code violations?+

Yes. Code violations are common, especially on older or vacant properties in Brunswick. We buy properties with active code violations and handle the remediation after closing. You do not need to fix anything before selling.

Absolutely. A new roof is one of the most common — and most expensive — repairs homeowners face. We buy houses that need roofs, foundations, HVAC systems, plumbing, electrical, and everything else. You do not spend a dime on repairs.

Is your offer guaranteed?+

Yes. Unlike traditional buyers whose offers are contingent on inspections, appraisals, and financing, our cash offer is firm. Once you accept, the deal is done — no renegotiation, no backing out, no surprises at the closing table.

Do you buy rental properties with tenants in place?+

Yes. We purchase rental properties with existing tenants, vacant units, and everything in between. You do not need to evict tenants before selling — we handle the transition after closing.

What if my house has liens or back taxes?+

We regularly buy properties with liens, back taxes, and title issues in Brunswick. These complications are handled at closing — the liens are paid from the sale proceeds, and you receive the remaining balance. Our title team navigates these situations every day.
