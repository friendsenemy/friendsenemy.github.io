  • Facing Foreclosure: California allows non-judicial foreclosure, which can move approximately 120 days. We can close before the auction date and stop the process.

  • Property Damage or Code Violations: Fire damage, water damage, mold, structural issues — we buy properties that traditional buyers won't touch.
