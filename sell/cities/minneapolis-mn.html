Situations We Help Minneapolis Homeowners With

  • Facing Foreclosure: Minnesota allows non-judicial foreclosure, which can move approximately 6 months. We can close before the auction date and stop the process.
  • Inherited Property: Minnesota probate is handled through the District Court. Formal probate typically takes 6-12 months. Minnesota allows an affidavit process for small estates under $75,000. We buy inherited homes as-is so you can settle the estate quickly.

  • Liens or Title Issues: Tax liens, judgment liens, and title problems are common in Minneapolis. We work with title companies to resolve these at closing.
  • Divorce or Relocation: When you need to sell quickly due to life changes, we provide certainty — a cash offer you can count on.
  • Property Damage or Code Violations: Fire damage, water damage, mold, structural issues — we buy properties that traditional buyers won't touch.
