## PART 1: HOW THIS SYSTEM CAPTURES SELLERS BEFORE AGENTS AND INVESTORS

### The Core Insight: Sellers Research Before They Call Anyone

The average distressed seller Googles their situation **17+ times** before picking up the phone or filling out a form. The average inherited property heir spends **4–8 weeks** researching before contacting anyone.

**The window:** If you can be the most helpful, trustworthy resource they find during that research phase, you become their first call — not your competition.
