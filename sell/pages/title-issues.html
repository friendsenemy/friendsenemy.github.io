Liens, Title Problems, Code Violations?
We Buy Properties Others Won't.

Title problems stop the traditional real estate market cold. We specialize in buying properties with complex title issues — and resolving them as part of the process.

Types of Title Issues We Handle

Most real estate agents will tell you to "clean up" a title before they'll list it — which can take months and cost thousands. We work through title problems differently.

🔴 Tax Liens & IRS Liens

Back property taxes or federal tax liens don't have to stop you from selling. These are typically paid off at closing from the sale proceeds. We've helped dozens of sellers in exactly this situation.

🔴 Judgment Liens

Court judgments against you that attach to your property can be resolved at closing. We work with title companies that specialize in negotiating and satisfying judgment liens.

🔴 Missing Heirs / Cloudy Title

Properties with gaps in ownership history — missing signatures, unknown heirs, or old deed errors — need specialized title work. We have relationships with title attorneys who handle these cases.

🔴 HOA Liens

Unpaid HOA dues create liens that must be resolved before title transfers. We factor this into our offer and handle the resolution.

🔴 Mechanics Liens

If contractors were never paid, they may have filed a mechanics lien. These are usually negotiable and we handle the resolution as part of the purchase.

🔴 Code Violations & Unpermitted Work

Cities issue fines and violations that can cloud a title. We buy properties with open code violations and work with local municipalities to resolve them post-purchase.

Our Process for Title Problems

  1. You tell us about the property and what you know about the title issues
  2. We order a preliminary title search (at our cost)
  3. We give you an honest assessment of what can be resolved and how
  4. We make an offer that accounts for resolution costs
  5. Our title company handles clearing the title for closing

You don't pay anything upfront. Resolution costs come out of the purchase price, not your pocket.
