Selling Land?
We Buy Raw Land, Lots, and Acreage Fast.

The land market is slow, fragmented, and dominated by brokers who charge high commissions for poor results. We buy land directly — any size, any condition, any county.

Why Land Is Hard to Sell Traditionally

Land sits on the market far longer than homes — often 12–36 months or more. The reasons:

  • Fewer buyers than for homes
  • Most buyers need land loans (hard to get, high rates)
  • Lack of comparable sales data makes pricing difficult
  • Utilities, access, and zoning complicate buyer interest
  • Land brokers are few and charge 10%+ commissions

Types of Land We Buy

  • ✅ Vacant residential lots
  • ✅ Raw rural acreage
  • ✅ Timber and agricultural land
  • ✅ Land with back taxes or liens
  • ✅ Inherited or probate land
  • ✅ Landlocked parcels
  • ✅ Land with access or easement issues
  • ✅ Commercial or industrial lots
  • ✅ Infill lots in urban areas

What We Look At

When evaluating a land offer, we consider:

  • Location and access (road frontage, easements)
  • Zoning and permitted uses
  • Size in acres or square feet
  • Utilities available or nearby
  • Back taxes or liens owed
  • Recent comparable land sales in the area

We'll do our research and give you a fair offer — typically within 48 hours of receiving your information.
