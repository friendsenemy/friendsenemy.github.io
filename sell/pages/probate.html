Frequently Asked Questions

Can I sell the property if it's still in probate? +
In many cases, yes. The executor or administrator of the estate can often sell property during probate with court approval. We can help guide you through this process.
What if there are multiple heirs who disagree? +
We work with all parties to find a solution. A cash sale often simplifies things since proceeds can be split easily among heirs.
Do I need to clean out the house first? +
No. We buy properties as-is, including any furniture, belongings, or debris inside. We'll handle the cleanout.
What if there are back taxes owed on the property? +
We can still buy the property. Outstanding taxes or liens are typically resolved at closing from the sale proceeds.
I live out of state. Can you still buy the property? +
Absolutely. Many of our sellers are out of state. We handle all local inspections, paperwork, and logistics.
