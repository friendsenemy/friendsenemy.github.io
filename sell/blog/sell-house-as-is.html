What "As-Is" Actually Means

An as-is sale means you're selling the property in its current condition, without making repairs or improvements before closing. You still must disclose known material defects ГўВЂВ” as-is doesn't mean you can hide problems. It simply means you're not agreeing to fix anything as part of the deal.
