  • Facing Foreclosure: Texas non-judicial foreclosure moves in as little as 21 days. We can close before the first-Tuesday trustee sale and stop the process.

How does Texas foreclosure timing affect selling my house?

Texas is one of the fastest foreclosure states in the country. After a Notice of Default, a trustee sale can happen in as little as 21 days on the first Tuesday of the month. If you are facing foreclosure in Texas, time is critical — we can close before the auction date and stop the process.
