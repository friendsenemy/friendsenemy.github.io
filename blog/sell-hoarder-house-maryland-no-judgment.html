Why a Cash Sale Makes Sense Here

The traditional listing path doesn't work for hoarder properties. Here's why almost every Maryland real estate agent will turn down the listing or insist on a major cleanout first:

  • MLS photos are required. Listing agents need 15+ interior photos that get pushed to Zillow, Realtor.com, Redfin, and dozens of syndicated sites. Those photos live online forever.
  • Open houses bring traffic. Buyers, neighbors, and lookers walk through. Word spreads.
  • Buyer financing requires inspections. FHA, VA, and most conventional loans require an appraiser inside. If the appraiser can't access rooms or sees structural concerns under the contents, the loan dies.
  • Insurance and liability issues. Some agents won't show a heavily packed house due to fall risk, biohazard concerns, or pest exposure.
