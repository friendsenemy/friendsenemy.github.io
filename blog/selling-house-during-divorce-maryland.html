Divorce is already one of life's most difficult transitions. Adding a home sale to the process — with agents, showings, negotiations, and months of waiting — makes everything harder. For Maryland couples going through a divorce, selling the marital home to a cash buyer can simplify the process, speed up the settlement, and let both parties move forward.

Maryland Divorce and Property Division

Maryland is an equitable distribution state, which means marital property is divided fairly — though not necessarily equally — by the court. The marital home is typically the largest shared asset, and both parties need to agree on what happens to it. The three most common options are: one spouse buys out the other, the home is sold and proceeds are split, or the home is kept temporarily for the benefit of children.

When both parties want to sell, a cash sale is often the fastest and least contentious option. There are no real estate agents taking a 5-6% commission, no staging or repair costs, and no uncertainty about whether a buyer's financing will fall through.

Why Cash Sales Work for Divorcing Couples

Speed and certainty are what matter most during a divorce. A traditional home sale can take three to six months — time that keeps both parties financially and emotionally tied to each other. A cash sale with Pages of Purpose LLC can close in as little as 7 to 14 days.

  • Both parties agree to sell — We make a fair offer, both spouses sign off, and the proceeds are distributed according to the settlement agreement.
